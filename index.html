What energizes me most is the moment a messy business problem turns into a clean data model — and the moment that model turns back into a decision someone can actually act on. I want to keep building toward that space, often called an analytics translator: technical enough to build and evaluate the model myself, business-fluent enough to make it matter. That's what pulls me toward Data & Analytics Consulting.
