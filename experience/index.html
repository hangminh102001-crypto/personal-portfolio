BI Dashboard Delivery — End-to-End Process

  1. Discovery — meet with the business department to understand their process, survey reporting needs, inventory existing data sources, and collect data samples from all relevant working files.
  2. Business modeling — analyze the data samples to identify Enums (categories), Entities, and Transactions; build a report mockup (Canva / Figma / Power BI Desktop) and iterate with the business until it's approved.
  3. Dimensional design — translate the business model into Dimension and Fact tables; work with Data Engineers to load them into a staging database and build the ETL pipeline.
  4. Staging build — build the Power BI report against the staging database and construct the visuals.
  5. Production access request — document the required data scope (systems, tables, fields) and its business value, then submit an access request copying the relevant business owner and IT manager — via IMPORTRANGE-linked Google Sheets for spreadsheet sources, or a ticket to the data engineering team for system sources.
  6. Go-live switch — once access is granted, the pipeline is switched from staging to production, the report is repointed to the production dataset, and it's published to the Power BI Service.
  7. Rollout & support — share the report with business users (Power BI Pro license required), set permissions, run user training and UAT, then collect feedback and fix issues.
